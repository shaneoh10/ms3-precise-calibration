About Us

Precise Calibration Ltd. are an Irish based Instrumentation Contracting company and since our formation we have earned a reputation for quality workmanship and service . We provide tailored calibration management and instrumentation solutions to clients across Oil and Gas, Medical Device, Pharmaceutical, Manufacturing and Data Center sectors in both Ireland and abroad.

We are ISO Quality assured to the latest standard and NSAI Certified.
